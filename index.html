When is the Open Pipe Kit not a good option?

  • You can't run a power chord to where you need to collect data and you can't afford a rechargeable battery and a solar panel.
  • You can't get WiFi or an Ethernet chord to where you need to.
  • You can't find a USB sensor that collects the data you need and you don't know how to solder together something that would work.

If any of the above are true, then an OPK as a gateway might be a good option for you. We're still working out how that would work exactly so please join us for our weekly OPK Hangout on Thursdays at 7pm EDT at [this Google Hangout](https://plus.google.com/hangouts/_/rjsteinert.com).
